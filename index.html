Drywall Services

H Drywall, LLC provides complete drywall services for homeowners, property managers and contractors across Oahu.

Drywall repair for holes and cracks

Clean repairs for dents, door knob holes, nail pops and damaged corners so your walls look like nothing ever happened.

Water damage and leak repairs

Cut out wet or mold damaged drywall, replace insulation if needed and restore the area so it is ready for paint and inspection.

Texture matching and Level 5 finish

Match existing orange peel, knockdown or custom textures, or upgrade to smooth Level 5 finish for high end lighting conditions.

Ceiling repair and patches

Repair sagging, cracked or damaged ceilings, fix previous patch work and prepare the surface for paint or new fixtures.

Painting prep and prime ready surfaces

Surfaces sanded, cleaned and primed so your painter can move in quickly and achieve an even, long lasting finish.

New drywall installation

Straight, clean hanging and finishing for additions, remodels and light commercial spaces with attention to framing corrections and details.

Additional services

  • Patch and repair drywall for water damage, stress cracks, and more
  • Non asbestos popcorn ceiling removal
  • Interior renovations, install or remove non structural partition walls
  • Steel stud framing, non structural framing for commercial spaces
  • Drywall installation and finishing, all textures and smooth wall levels
  • Acoustic grid ceiling installation, fire rated and non fire rated options
  • Insulation installation for energy efficiency
