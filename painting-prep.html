Drywall finish levels for paint

Different projects call for different finish levels. We help you choose what makes sense for your space and budget on Oahu.

Level 4 finish

A high quality standard finish for most homes and offices. Joints and fasteners are coated, sanded and the surface is ready for primer and paint.

Level 5 finish

Skim coat applied to create an ultra smooth surface, ideal for areas with strong lighting or dark paint colors.

Spot repairs and blending

When only parts of a room need work, we blend repairs into existing surfaces so the final paint job looks consistent.

Our painting prep process

We coordinate with you and your painter so the schedule and expectations are clear before work starts.

Inspect and plan

We walk through each room, note repairs, lighting conditions and paint plans, then recommend the right level of finish.

Repair, skim and sand

Cracks, dents and seams are repaired, then skim coated and sanded to achieve a consistent surface.

Ready for primer and paint

When we are done, your painter can move in and start priming without extra drywall prep work.
